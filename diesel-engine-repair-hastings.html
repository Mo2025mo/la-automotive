Diesel Engine Maintenance Tips

Keep Your Diesel Engine Healthy

  1. Regular DPF Regeneration: Take occasional motorway drives
  2. Quality Fuel: Use premium diesel with additives
  3. Service Intervals: Don't extend service periods
  4. Warning Lights: Address DPF warnings immediately
  5. Warm-Up Time: Allow engine to warm before hard driving
  6. Cool-Down: Let turbo cool after hard driving
