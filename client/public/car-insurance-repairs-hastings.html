Common Insurance Questions

Will my insurance cover the repairs?

Coverage depends on your policy type and circumstances. We provide detailed estimates to help your insurer assess the claim quickly.

How long do insurance repairs take?

Simple repairs: 2-5 days. Complex collision damage: 1-2 weeks. We provide realistic timeframes and regular updates.

Do I need to pay anything upfront?

For approved insurance claims, we handle direct billing. You may only need to pay your policy excess.

Can I choose my own repairer?

Yes, most insurance policies allow you to choose your preferred repair center. We're approved by all major insurers.
