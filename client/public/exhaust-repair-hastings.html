Signs Your Exhaust Needs Repair

  • Loud exhaust noise - Unusual or excessive noise from exhaust
  • Rattling sounds - Loose or damaged exhaust components
  • Visible damage - Rust, holes, or hanging exhaust parts
  • Exhaust smoke - Blue, white, or black smoke from tailpipe
  • Reduced fuel economy - Poor exhaust flow affects efficiency
  • Smell in cabin - Exhaust fumes entering vehicle interior
  • Failed emissions test - MOT failure on emissions levels
  • Loss of power - Blocked exhaust affecting performance
  • Vibrations - Unusual vibrations when accelerating
  • Check engine light - Engine management warnings

MOT Emissions Requirements

Your exhaust system must meet strict emissions standards for MOT compliance:

  • Petrol vehicles - CO and hydrocarbon limits
  • Diesel vehicles - Smoke opacity requirements
  • Catalytic converter - Must be present and functioning
  • DPF systems - Diesel particulate filters must be intact
