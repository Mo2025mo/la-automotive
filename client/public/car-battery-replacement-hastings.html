Signs Your Battery Needs Replacement

  • Slow engine cranking - Engine turns over slowly when starting
  • Dashboard warning lights - Battery or charging system warnings
  • Dim headlights - Lights dimmer than usual, especially when idling
  • Frequent jump starts - Needing jump starts regularly
  • Battery age - Battery over 4-5 years old
  • Swollen battery case - Physical damage or swelling visible
  • Corrosion - White/blue powder around terminals
  • Electrical problems - Radio, lights, or accessories malfunctioning
  • Check engine light - Can indicate charging system problems
  • Complete failure - Car won't start at all

Battery Maintenance Tips

  • Regular driving - Drive regularly to keep battery charged
  • Turn off electronics - Switch off lights and accessories when engine off
  • Keep terminals clean - Clean corrosion from battery terminals
  • Check connections - Ensure terminals are tight and secure
  • Avoid deep discharge - Don't let battery go completely flat
  • Temperature protection - Park in garage during extreme weather
  • Regular testing - Have battery tested annually after 3 years
