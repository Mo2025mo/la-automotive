Emergency Car Problems We Fix

Common Emergency Situations:

  • Car won't start - battery, starter, or electrical problems
  • Engine overheating - coolant leaks, thermostat, radiator issues
  • Brake problems - spongy pedal, grinding, brake failure

  • Steering problems - difficulty steering, unusual vibrations
  • Exhaust issues - loud noises, smoke, emissions problems
  • Electrical failures - lights, indicators, charging system
  • Fuel system problems - engine cutting out, poor performance

When to Call for Emergency Repair

Call immediately if you experience:

  • Any safety concerns about driving your vehicle
  • Sudden loss of braking performance
  • Engine overheating or steam from bonnet
  • Unusual noises that developed suddenly
  • Warning lights that won't clear
  • Steering problems or vibrations
  • Complete breakdown or failure to start
