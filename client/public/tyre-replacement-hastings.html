Signs You Need New Tyres

  • Tread depth below 3mm - Legal limit is 1.6mm but replace at 3mm for safety
  • Sidewall damage - Cuts, bulges, or cracking in the sidewall
  • Uneven wear patterns - May indicate alignment or suspension issues
  • Age over 6 years - Even with good tread, rubber deteriorates over time
  • Vibration while driving - Could indicate internal damage or imbalance
  • Frequent punctures - Worn tyres are more susceptible to damage
